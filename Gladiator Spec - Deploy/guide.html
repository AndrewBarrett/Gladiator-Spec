Garrow's Gladiator Spec Guide

Basic Abilities

An important start point is to define the core identity/fantasy of the spec and with Gladiator that's relatively simple. The fantasy of a sword-and-board is using the shield as an offensive weapon in tandem with a one hander and not in a purely defensive role. The first core ability is Shield Charge.

Shield Charge

2 Charges
12 Second CD
10 Yard Range
Generates 20 Rage
Deals X damage to your primary target and 50% damage to all other targets in front of the warrior.
Increases the damage and critical strike chance of your next ability by 20%.

Shield Charge is intended to fit right into the theme of using the shield as an offensive weapon in combo with other abilities, striking the targets infront of the warrior and leaving them vulnerable to attacks following it.

Riposte

8 Second CD
20 Rage
Deals X physical damage to all targets in a line infront of the warrior and places a bleed dot on them.
The damage from subsequent casts stacks.

Smacked the enemy 'round the noggin with the shield? Now stick 'em with the pointy end.

A number of other abilities deal extra damage based on how much Riposte damage is left on the target. E.g. Execute will deal X damage + 40% of Riposte's remaining damage. The Riposte dot represents the injuries a Gladiator might inflict on his or her opponent during a brutal fight and abilities such as Execute follow up to finish the job.

Execute

30 Rage
Deals X physical damage to the target and causes Riposte to instantly deal 40% of its remaining damage.

Shield Crash

Deals X physical damage to all targets infront of the warrior.
For the next 6 seconds Riposte, Execute, and Cleave have no cooldown and cost 50% less rage.
Allows you to combo different attacks together to enable powerful abilities.

After using Shield Crash it places a short buff on the player intended to allow Riposte, Execute, and Cleave to be cast in quick succesion.

Impale

Casting Riposte x2 and then Cleave during Shield Crash allows the use of Impale.
A channeled 3 second cast stunning enemies in a line infront of the warrior and dealing heavy damage.
The damage of Impale is increased by a percentage of all damage caused while the Shield Crash buff is active.

Decapitate

Casting Riposte x2 and then Execute during Shield Crash allows the use of Decapitate.
An empowered version of Execute.
The damage of Decapitate is increased by a percentage of all damage caused while the Shield Crash buff is active.

Heroic Strike

30 Rage
Deals X damage to the target.
Not on the GCD.

Devastate

Deals X damage to the target.
Reduces the rage cost of Heroic Strike by 5 rage for 15 seconds.
Stacks up to 6 times and subsequent casts do not refresh the duration.
